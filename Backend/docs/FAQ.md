# JobSpy FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

## Account and Registration

### Q: How do I create a JobSpy account?
A: Visit the registration page, enter your email address, create a password, and verify your email. You can also sign up using Google or LinkedIn.

### Q: What information do I need to provide?
A: At minimum, you need an email address and password. Additional profile information (name, location, skills) is optional but recommended for better job matches.

### Q: Can I change my email address?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Account > Change Email. You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

### Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?
A: Each email address can have one account. If you need a separate account, use a different email address.

### Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

## Job Search and Filtering

### Q: How do I search for jobs?
A: Use the search bar to enter job titles, keywords, or company names. Use filters to narrow results by location, salary, job type, and more.

### Q: What search operators are supported?
A: We support quotes for exact phrases ("machine learning"), minus for exclusion (python -junior), and OR for multiple options (New York OR Remote).

### Q: Can I search by salary range?
A: Yes, use the Salary filter to set minimum and maximum salary expectations. Jobs matching your range will be displayed.

### Q: How do I filter by location?
A: Use the Location filter to search by city, state, or select "Remote" for remote positions. You can select multiple locations.

### Q: Can I save my search?
A: Yes, after applying filters, click "Save Search" to save your search criteria. Access saved searches from the sidebar.

### Q: How do I sort search results?
A: Click the Sort dropdown to choose from Most Recent, Most Relevant, Salary High to Low, Salary Low to High, or Company Rating.

### Q: What does "Most Relevant" mean?
A: Most Relevant sorts jobs based on your profile, skills, and job preferences to show the best matches first.

## Saved Jobs and Collections

### Q: How do I save a job?
A: Click the heart icon on any job card or open the job and click "Save Job". The job will be added to your Saved Jobs.

### Q: Can I organize saved jobs?
A: Yes, create collections to organize jobs by category (e.g., "Dream Companies", "Backup Options"). Drag and drop jobs into collections.

### Q: How do I delete a saved job?
A: Click the heart icon again to unsave, or open the job and click "Delete from Saved". The job will be removed from your saved list.

### Q: Can I compare jobs?
A: Yes, select 2-4 jobs and click "Compare" to view them side-by-side and compare salary, benefits, location, and requirements.

### Q: Can I add notes to saved jobs?
A: Yes, open a saved job and click "Add Note" to write personal observations. Notes are searchable and sortable.

### Q: Can I rate jobs?
A: Yes, open a saved job and use the star rating (1-5) to rate the job. Ratings help you prioritize opportunities.

## Alerts and Notifications

### Q: How do I create a job alert?
A: Go to Alerts > Create New Alert, configure your criteria (job title, location, salary, etc.), and choose notification frequency.

### Q: What notification methods are available?
A: Email (free), In-app notifications (free), and SMS (premium feature).

### Q: How often will I receive alerts?
A: Choose from Real-time (as jobs are posted), Daily digest, or Weekly summary.

### Q: Can I have multiple alerts?
A: Yes, create as many alerts as you need for different job types or locations.

### Q: How do I edit an alert?
A: Go to Alerts, find your alert, and click "Edit" to modify criteria or notification frequency.

### Q: Can I pause an alert?
A: Yes, click "Pause" to temporarily disable an alert without deleting it. Click "Resume" to reactivate.

### Q: How do I delete an alert?
A: Click "Delete" on the alert. You can recreate it anytime.

### Q: Why aren't I receiving alerts?
A: Check your notification settings, verify your email address, check spam folder, and ensure alert criteria aren't too restrictive.

## Export and Data

### Q: Can I export my saved jobs?
A: Yes, go to Saved Jobs, select jobs, and click "Export". Choose CSV, PDF, JSON, or Excel format.

### Q: Can I export search results?
A: Yes, after searching, click "Export Results" to download current search results in your preferred format.

### Q: Can I schedule automatic exports?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Exports > Schedule Export. Choose format, frequency, and delivery method.

### Q: What formats are supported?
A: CSV (spreadsheets), PDF (printing), JSON (data analysis), and Excel (advanced spreadsheets).

### Q: Can I export my profile data?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Data Export > Export Profile to download your profile information.

### Q: How do I import data from another job site?
A: Currently, we don't support direct imports. You can manually add jobs or use our API for bulk operations.

## Profile and Preferences

### Q: How do I update my profile?
A: Go to Profile > Edit Profile to update personal information, add skills, experience, education, and certifications.

### Q: Can I make my profile private?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Privacy > Profile Visibility to make your profile private or public.

### Q: Can I hide my salary expectations?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Privacy to control whether your salary expectations are visible.

### Q: How do I add skills to my profile?
A: Go to Profile > Skills and add skills relevant to your job search. Skills are searchable by employers.

### Q: Can I upload a profile photo?
A: Yes, go to Profile > Edit Profile and click "Upload Photo". Use a professional headshot.

### Q: How do I set job preferences?
A: Go to Profile > Job Preferences to set preferred job titles, locations, salary, job types, and experience level.

## Settings and Notifications

### Q: How do I change notification settings?
A: Go to Settings > Notifications to enable/disable email, in-app, or SMS notifications.

### Q: Can I set quiet hours?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Notifications > Quiet Hours to set times when notifications are muted.

### Q: How do I enable dark mode?
A: Go to Settings > Display > Theme and select "Dark Mode".

### Q: Can I change the language?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Display > Language to choose your preferred language.

### Q: How do I manage active sessions?
A: Go to Settings > Security > Active Sessions to view and log out of other sessions.

### Q: Can I enable two-factor authentication?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication to enable 2FA for added security.

## Technical Issues

### Q: The site is loading slowly. What can I do?
A: Try clearing your browser cache, disabling browser extensions, or using a different browser. Contact support if issues persist.

### Q: I'm getting an error message. What should I do?
A: Note the error message and contact support with details. Include your browser type and version.

### Q: Jobs aren't loading. How do I fix this?
A: Refresh the page, clear browser cache, or try a different browser. Check your internet connection.

### Q: Search isn't working. What's wrong?
A: Try clearing filters and searching again. Ensure your search terms aren't too specific.

### Q: Can I use JobSpy on mobile?
A: Yes, JobSpy is fully responsive and works on all devices. Download our mobile app for the best experience.

### Q: What browsers are supported?
A: We support Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Use the latest version for best performance.
