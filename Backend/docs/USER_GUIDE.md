## Advanced Search

### Using Search Operators

JobSpy supports advanced search operators for precise job discovery:

- **Exact phrase**: Use quotes to search for exact phrases
  - Example: `"machine learning engineer"`
  
- **Exclude terms**: Use minus sign to exclude jobs
  - Example: `python -junior`
  
- **Multiple locations**: Search across multiple cities
  - Example: `New York OR San Francisco OR Remote`

### Search Filters

Apply multiple filters simultaneously:

1. **Job Title**: Filter by specific job titles or keywords
2. **Company**: Search for jobs from specific companies
3. **Location**: Filter by city, state, or remote options
4. **Salary Range**: Set minimum and maximum salary expectations
5. **Job Type**: Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Freelance
6. **Experience Level**: Entry-level, Mid-level, Senior, Executive
7. **Industry**: Filter by industry sector
8. **Posted Date**: Find recently posted jobs

### Saved Searches

Save your frequently used search queries:

1. Click "Save Search" after applying filters
2. Give your search a memorable name
3. Access saved searches from the sidebar
4. Edit or delete saved searches anytime

## Filtering and Sorting

### Sort Options

- **Most Recent**: Newest jobs first (default)
- **Most Relevant**: Based on your profile and preferences
- **Salary High to Low**: Highest paying jobs first
- **Salary Low to High**: Most affordable opportunities
- **Company Rating**: Highest rated companies first

### Advanced Filtering

Combine multiple filters for precise results:

1. Apply primary filters (location, job type)
2. Add secondary filters (salary, experience level)
3. Use search operators for additional refinement
4. Save the combination for future use

### Filter Presets

Create custom filter presets:

1. Configure your ideal job criteria
2. Click "Save as Preset"
3. Name your preset (e.g., "Remote Senior Roles")
4. Apply presets with one click

## Saved Jobs Management

### Organizing Saved Jobs

Create collections to organize your saved jobs:

1. Click "Create Collection" in Saved Jobs
2. Name your collection (e.g., "Dream Companies", "Backup Options")
3. Drag and drop jobs into collections
4. Reorder collections by priority

### Bulk Actions

Manage multiple saved jobs efficiently:

1. Select multiple jobs using checkboxes
2. **Move to Collection**: Move selected jobs to a collection
3. **Add Tags**: Tag jobs for easy filtering
4. **Delete**: Remove jobs from saved list
5. **Export**: Export selected jobs to CSV

### Job Comparison

Compare multiple jobs side-by-side:

1. Select 2-4 jobs to compare
2. Click "Compare" button
3. View salary, benefits, location, and requirements
4. Make informed decisions

### Notes and Ratings

Add personal notes to saved jobs:

1. Open a saved job
2. Click "Add Note" to write personal observations
3. Rate the job (1-5 stars)
4. Notes are searchable and sortable

## Alert Configuration

### Creating Smart Alerts

Set up alerts that match your job preferences:

1. Go to Alerts section
2. Click "Create New Alert"
3. Configure alert criteria:
   - Job title keywords
   - Locations (single or multiple)
   - Salary range
   - Job type
   - Experience level
4. Set notification frequency:
   - Real-time (as jobs are posted)
   - Daily digest
   - Weekly summary
5. Choose notification method:
   - Email
   - In-app notification
   - SMS (premium feature)

### Alert Management

Manage your active alerts:

1. View all active alerts in the Alerts dashboard
2. **Edit Alert**: Modify criteria or frequency
3. **Pause Alert**: Temporarily disable without deleting
4. **Delete Alert**: Remove alert permanently
5. **Test Alert**: Send test notification

### Alert Statistics

Track alert performance:

- Total jobs matched
- Jobs saved from alert
- Most common job titles
- Most common locations
- Alert effectiveness score

- `Esc`: Close modals/dialogs

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Q: Why aren't my alerts working?**
- Check notification settings
- Verify email address is correct
- Check spam folder for emails
- Ensure alert criteria are not too restrictive

**Q: How do I export my data?**
- Go to Settings > Data Export
- Choose format and date range
- Click Export
- File will be downloaded

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
- Go to Settings > Account
- Click "Change Email"
- Verify new email address
- Old email will no longer be used

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
- Go to Settings > Privacy
- Click "Delete Account"
- Confirm deletion
- All data will be permanently removed
